The Atelier

Craftsmanship

I Materials

Only grade-one alloys, hand-selected sapphires, and single-origin precious metals pass our procurement threshold.

II Precision

Each movement is calibrated to ±2 seconds per day. Every joint is fitted to tolerances measured in microns.

III Time

A single YNG timepiece requires over 400 hours of atelier work from first sketch to final polish.

IV Legacy

We build for generations. Every piece carries a discrete serial mark and a certificate of provenance.
